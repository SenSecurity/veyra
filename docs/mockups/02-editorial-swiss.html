Veyra & the Spoken Word

Saturday, 3 May 2026
Esch-sur-Alzette · LU

A small Windows tool that listens, transcribes, and politely composes your correspondence — entirely on your own machine.

— The Cover Story —

Press F24. Speak. Watch the words appear.

Veyra runs quietly in the background until summoned. A single hotkey opens a microphone; a second one stops it. In the space between, the tool writes down what was said and pastes it where the cursor was waiting — no cloud, no upload, no detour through somebody else's server. The whole apparatus, model included, lives on disk.
